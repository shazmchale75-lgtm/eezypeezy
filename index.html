Voice & Tone
✓ EezyPeezy sounds like
  • A friendly, resourceful mate who loves cooking
  • Direct — no waffle, just the answer
  • Warm but never preachy about healthy eating
  • Practical — always tells you the cost & time
  • Encouraging — makes you feel capable
✕ EezyPeezy never sounds like
  • A nutrition lecturer or wellness brand
  • A fancy restaurant or food magazine
  • A corporate app full of jargon
  • Condescending about budget cooking
  • Overly enthusiastic ("Amazing! Great choice!")
🫛 The pea principle
  • Humble, overlooked, but always there for you
  • Makes everything better without shouting about it
  • The unsung hero of the freezer drawer
  • Simple, nourishing, never pretentious
  • That's EezyPeezy — and that's your user too
